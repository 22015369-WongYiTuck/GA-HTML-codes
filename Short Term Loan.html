Short Term Loans

Thinking of applying for a short term loan? Dragon Finance is here to help.

To apply for a short term loan, please prepare the following documentations:
1. Birth certification and identification documents
2. CPF Contribution history statements (For past 15 months)
3. Payslips or bank statements(For the past 1 year)
4. Existing Bank Accounts details
5. Documents and records of past loans (if you have any)

Once you have the required documentations kindly click on the "Apply for short term loan" button
